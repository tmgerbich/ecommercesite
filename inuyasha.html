Inuyasha

Inuyasha follows Kagome Higurashi, a modern-day girl who is transported to the feudal era of Japan through a magical well. There, she meets Inuyasha, a half-demon who seeks the Shikon Jewel to become a full demon. Together, they embark on a quest to retrieve the scattered shards of the jewel while battling demons and other adversaries. Filled with action, romance, and supernatural elements, the series explores themes of love, friendship, and the clash between the past and present.
