Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to combat curses after he swallows a powerful cursed object. Filled with intense battles, supernatural elements, and compelling characters, the series blends action, horror, and dark fantasy.

HunterxHunter

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who aspires to become a Hunter like his father. Hunters are elite individuals who specialize in various fields, from treasure hunting to assassination. Alongside his friends, Gon embarks on a dangerous journey to pass the rigorous Hunter Exam, facing formidable opponents and uncovering hidden truths about his past. With its intricate world-building, complex characters, and thrilling adventures, the series explores themes of friendship, determination, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Bleach

Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper after encountering Rukia Kuchiki. He protects the living from evil spirits and guides departed souls to the afterlife. The series is known for its intense battles, diverse characters, and rich supernatural lore.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan depicts a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the mysteries surrounding their existence. With intense action, complex characters, and deep themes, the series explores the struggle for survival and the cost of freedom in a world on the brink of extinction.

Inuyasha

Inuyasha follows Kagome Higurashi, a modern-day girl who is transported to the feudal era of Japan through a magical well. There, she meets Inuyasha, a half-demon who seeks the Shikon Jewel to become a full demon. Together, they embark on a quest to retrieve the scattered shards of the jewel while battling demons and other adversaries. Filled with action, romance, and supernatural elements, the series explores themes of love, friendship, and the clash between the past and present.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons, leaving only his sister Nezuko, who has been turned into a demon. Determined to turn her back into a human and seek revenge, Tanjiro embarks on a perilous journey, facing powerful demons and forging bonds with fellow demon slayers. With breathtaking animation and heartfelt storytelling, the series explores themes of family, determination, and the struggle between good and evil.
