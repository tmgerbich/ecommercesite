Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who aspires to become a Hunter like his father. Hunters are elite individuals who specialize in various fields, from treasure hunting to assassination. Alongside his friends, Gon embarks on a dangerous journey to pass the rigorous Hunter Exam, facing formidable opponents and uncovering hidden truths about his past. With its intricate world-building, complex characters, and thrilling adventures, the series explores themes of friendship, determination, and the pursuit of one's dreams.
